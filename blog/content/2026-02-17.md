Just a quick update today. The new outdoor monitor is up and running. I'm not very pleased with the location though, as it's sitting on a window ledge from my apartment. The area is shielded well from road pollution as it's sits in between two buildings. However, the problem is there seems to be a micro-climate and the temperature is much lower than if it were out in the open. This throws the humidity off too because there isn't much breeze. I'd like to reloacte it but I currently don't have an alternative way to power it. I may look into solar power soon and using LoRa radio to communicate with the server if it's too far from my WiFi.

## About the sensors
- **PlantTower PMS5003:** 1.0, 2.5, and 10.0 particulate matter sensor.
- **BME280:** Temperature, humidity, and air pressure sensor.
- **M5Stack AtomS3 Dev:** This is the microntroller and it will be replaced as soon as I purchase a new ESP32. (My old ESP32s are dead, apparently.)

## Sensors update cylce  
Currently the PM sensor is updating every few seconds so you might see it changing wildly. I plan to setup a 10 minute average which should smooth this out a little bit. The temperature, humidity and air pressure should update every 60 seconds.
